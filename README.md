Notes . . .

  The client and server code come together.
  You may compile them separately on different machines.



Ready . . .

  shell> git clone https://github.com/spanishgum/tictactoe



Set . . .

  shell> cd path/to/tictactoe
  shell> make fresh



Go . . .

  client> ./client [server] [port]
  server> ./server [port]
